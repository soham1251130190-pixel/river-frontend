What We Monitor

Built for Real Detection

💧
TDS Monitoring
Total Dissolved Solids measured continuously. Detects chemical dumps, heavy metal contamination, and salinity spikes invisible to the naked eye.
🌡️
Temperature Sensing
Thermal pollution from industrial discharge tracked in real time. Sudden temperature spikes signal industrial cooling water release events.
⚗️
pH Analysis
Acid rain, agricultural runoff, and chemical spills shift river pH. Our calibrated sensors detect deviations from healthy 6.5–8.5 range.
🤖
ML Anomaly Detection
Isolation Forest model trained on CPCB baseline data. Flags abnormal reading combinations that individual thresholds would miss.
🔔
Instant WhatsApp Alerts
Pollution events trigger immediate WhatsApp alerts with reading values, anomaly score, and severity — within 60 seconds of detection.
🗺️
Live Map Dashboard
Colour-coded sensor nodes on an interactive map. Green means clean, red means act now. Designed for non-technical authorities.
